About company

PERSPEKTIVEN PAT DOOEL SKORJE company is ready to offer a wide range of household goods (expendable and packing materials, disposable tableware, household goods and other groups of household products). Our area of expertise also includes household garments and textile for home decoration and our customers are major Macedonian retailors of garments and textiles.

We established valuable connections with a number of top-class producers and are happy to offer a wide range of interior products: wallpaper, paints and coatings.

Our clients are both large and small companies in Macedonia and the Balkan region. Contact us and you will reduce your costs for searching, selecting, handling and delivering the goods you need for your business.

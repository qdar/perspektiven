TECHNISCHE TEXTILIEN LORRACH GmbH & Co. KG

Technische Textilien Lorrach company (Germany) produces special textiles including materials for furniture upholstery, mattresses, covers as well as materials for sewing ironing linen:

TULONA®

TULONA® industrial textiles are available as needle and nonwoven materials — felts made of various polymers such as polyester, polypropylene, polyacrylonitrile, aramid, PPS, polyimide, PVC, PTFE and viscose. Mixtures of fibers are also possible. After some additional chemical, thermal and surface treatments, the finished goods become well adaptable to usage conditions.
